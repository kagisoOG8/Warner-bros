VELMA


Director:Charlie Grandy


Cast:
Mindy Kaling, Constance Wu, Sam Richardson, Glenn Howerton, Jane Lynch, Melissa Fumero, Russell Peters, Wanda Sykes


Genre:
Animation


About:VELMA is an adult animated comedy series telling the origin story of Velma Dinkley, the unsung and underappreciated brains of the Scooby-Doo Mystery Inc. gang. An original and humorous spin that unmasks the complex and colorful past of one of America's most beloved mystery solvers, executive producer Mindy Kaling will voice the titular character. Charlie Grandy, Howard Klein and Sam Register also serve as executive producers. VELMA is produced by Warner Bros. Animation.
