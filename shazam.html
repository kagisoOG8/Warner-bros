Director:David F. Sandberg


Cast:

About:
From New Line Cinema comes “Shazam! Fury of the Gods,” which continues the story of teenage Billy Batson who, upon reciting the magic word “SHAZAM!,” is transformed into his adult Super Hero alter ego, Shazam.The film follows Billy and his fellow foster kids as they learn to balance teenage life with their superpowered personas. When a vengeful trio of ancient gods arrives on Earth seeking the magic stolen from them long ago, Shazam and his allies find themselves in a battle for their superpowers, their lives, and the fate of the world
